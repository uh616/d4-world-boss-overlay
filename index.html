Diablo 4 Helltide FAQ

How to use the Helltide Event Timer and Mystery Chest Tracker
To use the Helltide Tracker and Mystery Chest locator, watch the countdown on the website for the Helltide to begin. You can tap the Bell icon and adjust the audio slider to enable audio and browser notifications for Helltides and other events so you get alerted when the events begin. When the Helltide is active, the map of the Helltide zone will show up with the Mystery Chest locations, Ritual locations, and Event locations to help you efficiently navigate the area.
What are Mystery Chests?
Tortured Gifts of Mysteries are chests that spawn during Helltide events in Diablo 4. As of Season 6, they cost 250 Aberrant Cinders to open and have a chance to drop between 5-10 Legendary items (incl. Ancestral items and Uniques), Forgotten Souls, Angelbreath, Rawhide, Artificer’s Stones, Infernal Hordes Compass, Living Steel, Gem Fragments, Veiled Crystals, Gold, and Potions.
How many Mystery Chests spawn during a Helltide?
As of Season 6, two Mystery Chests are active during each Helltide (one per zone) in Scosglen, Dry Steppes, Fractured Peaks, Hawezar, and Nehantu, and three Mystery Chests are active in Kehjistan. Refer to the Helltide map for the Mystery Chest locations when the event is active.
How many Mystery Chests can you open during a Helltide event?
You can open each Mystery Chest once per Helltide. That means the most you can open is 2 per hour in all zones except Kehjistan, where you can open 3 per hour.
What is a Helltide?
A Helltide is a region-wide event in Diablo 4. Helltides feature empowered servants of Lilith with increased difficulty and loot. Helltides can occur in any of the six regions of Sanctuary in a predesignated set of zones. Monsters in a Helltide drop a currency called Cinders, which players can use to open Helltide Chests within the event zone. Dying will result in the player losing half of their Cinders.
When do Helltide events start in Diablo 4?
As of Season 6, Helltide events begin at the top of every hour and last 55 minutes. They are inactive for the remaining five minutes of the hour.
When do Helltide events start in Diablo 4?
Helltide events begin at the top of each hour. Each Helltide event lasts 55 minutes. See the Diablo 4 Helltides timer for the next Helltide start time.
Can Legion Events and World Bosses spawn in Helltide zones?
Yes, both Legion Events and World Bosses can spawn in Helltide regions. The monsters in these events will have an increased level.
What are Accursed Rituals?
Accursed Rituals are a summoning event located within any active Helltide region. Three Baneful Hearts are required to summon the Blood Maiden from Accursed Rituals. The Blood Maiden can drop Legendary Items, Lord Zir Mats, a chance for end-game bossing materials, etc. Players or players in a party with a player who contributes to summoning the Blood Miaden will receive bonus loot from killing the boss.
How to get Baneful Hearts in Diablo 4
Baneful Hearts can be acquired by opening Tortured Gifts of Mysteries and slaying Hellborn and Doomsayer variant monsters within the Helltide zones.
What are Realmwalkers?
Realmwalkers are part of an overworld Event called Hatred Rising, introduced in Season 6 of Diablo 4. These events begin every 15 minutes when a Realmwalker spawns and charges up before slowly walking to its sacred ground. Players must follow the Realmwalker, kill its Bloodbound Guardians, and defeat it to open a Seething Portal. Inside the Seething Portal is a dungeon players can complete to claim Seething Opals. Opals, when consumed, increase the drop chance of Equipment, Gold, Materials, or Boss Summoning items, depending on the Opal type.
